A hideout, by people who needed one too

Lighthouse Restaurant was born from a simple desire — to create a place where food, warmth, and ambiance converge into something magical. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into the city's most beloved dining destination.

We didn't want to build another restaurant. We wanted to build a pause — a moment of peace in a busy world, guided by great food and even better company.

Every ingredient is hand-selected, every recipe refined over years, and every plate presented with the care of someone cooking for family.
